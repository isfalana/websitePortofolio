Mobile Game SIBENI

SIBENI is an educational game that helps students learn Indonesian art and culture. SIBENI presents a regional clothing dress up game that aims to introduce the clothing to students, as well as features for learning musical instruments, regional songs and regional dances that are facilitated with sounds from each musical instrument and regional song and dance videos to provide knowledge of Indonesian culture for students.
